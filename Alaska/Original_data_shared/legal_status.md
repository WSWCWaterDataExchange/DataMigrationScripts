TWUP: Temporary Water Use Permit – for a period less than five years, rather than granting a true water right.

TWUA: Temporary Water Use Authorization -- we’ve morphed the twup’s into twua’s, mostly just semantics—more recent permits are twua’s. (doesn’t roll off the tongue as well as twup does!)

LAS: Land Administration System, overall it is a database used to maintain and track permits across the state.  Prior to 1982, permits of all kinds from the state were given an ADL number (Alaska Division of Lands), now they are given an LAS number.
